Data Deletion Instructions for InstraGraphAPI

If you wish to delete your data associated with InstraGraphAPI, please send an email to:

kasalmok@gmail.com

Use the subject line: “Delete My Data”.

All access tokens and related data will be permanently deleted.

Alternative Method

You may also revoke the app’s permissions directly from your Facebook account:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings
  2. Open “Security and Login”
  3. Choose “Apps and Websites”
  4. Find InstraGraphAPI
  5. Click “Remove”
