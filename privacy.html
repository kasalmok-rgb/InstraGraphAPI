Privacy Policy for InstraGraphAPI

InstraGraphAPI (“the Application”) collects and processes data strictly for the purpose of publishing content to Facebook Pages on behalf of the user. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Collected

  • Access tokens provided by Facebook
  • Basic page information required to publish videos
  • User-generated content (videos, descriptions)

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to upload videos to your Facebook Page. We do not store your data permanently.

Data Storage

All data is processed in real time and removed immediately after publishing.

Data Removal

You may request deletion of your data at any time via the Data Deletion page or by contacting us.
